Supplemental Figure 1. ARF protein alignments. Arabidopsis ARF17, ARF10, and ARF16 and Oryza sativa ARF10, NP_9222010, XP_466861, and XP_473206 proteins were aligned using Megalign (DNAStar, Madison, WI) using the Clustal W method. The DNA-binding domain (DBD) and C-terminal dimerization domain (III and IV) are noted. Prol, Ser, and Thr residues in the middle region are in red. miR160 complementary regions are highlighted in purple. Identical amino acids are highlighted in black and conserved amino acids are highlighted in gray boxes.
